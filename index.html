Meet the Baker

Naurah
Nafeesa

Pastry Chef & Founder

Naurah Nafeesa is a passionate and experienced baker whose love for pastry turned into a lifelong craft. A graduate of the prestigious Lavonne Academy of Baking Science and Pastry Arts in Bangalore, India, she has been creating extraordinary baked goods professionally since 2017 — first in India, and now bringing her expertise and heart to Dubai through The Dough Knot.
